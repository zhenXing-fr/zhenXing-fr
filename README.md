## 🌿 Featured projects

**The ones that tell a story.**

🪴 [**water_your_plant_agent**](https://github.com/zhenXing-fr/water_your_plant_agent) — I bought several Japanese maples. Every day I had to check weather forecasts and guess when to water them. So I built an agentic system that does it for me. A live playground for agent architecture, agent ops, and learning by shipping.

🔌 [**AvroDantic**](https://github.com/zhenXing-fr/AvroDantic) — At my previous company we built the data layer for EV charging networks (think Waze, but for electric cars). When IoT devices evolve their schemas mid-stream, your pipelines break. AvroDantic is the handshake layer I built to buffer that schema evolution between extract and transform.

⛵ [**smart_boat_sandbox**](https://github.com/zhenXing-fr/smart_boat_sandbox) — I made it to the final round for a startup building digital navigation systems for boats. They chose another candidate. This is how I would have designed the end-to-end solution — and how I would have interviewed the team I'd have hired.

🎤 [**hands_on_hiring**](https://github.com/zhenXing-fr/axway_interview_marketing_dummy_project) — As a lead, one of my most important jobs is building the team. I don't believe in algorithm puzzles. I design hands-on interviews based on real business scenarios, the kind of problem we'd actually work on together on day one.
